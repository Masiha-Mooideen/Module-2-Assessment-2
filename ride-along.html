RIDE ALONG 2

TITLE: RIDE ALONG 2

CAST: Ice Cube, Kevin Hart, Ken Jeong, Benjamin Bratt, Olivia Munn ,Bruce McGill, Tika Sumpter

AGE RESTRICTION: PG 13

SYNOPSIS:In the second film, crime lord Antonio Pope accuses Port Commissioner Griffin of stealing and orders a hitman to eliminate him. James and Ben, along with Detective Mayfield, bust drug dealer Troy. After a shootout, James captures Troy and finds a necklace/flash drive on him. Pope sends James to Miami to find the culprit. Ben plans his wedding and Angela calls James to help. They find A.J., who reveals that Pope is the real crook. They find A.J. at a club and find a bomb that explodes. Ben contacts his girlfriend Tasha to locate A.J. and brings him in on the investigation. They gather information at a party in Pope's mansion and use it to locate shipping trucks carrying Pope's contraband. Pope catches them and scolds them, but they realize it was a decoy. The team goes to a bar to discuss the situation, and Ben breaks free from cuffs. James, Maya, and A.J. catch Pope, but Ben arrives at the port to kill him.

DURATION:1 hour and 40 minutes
