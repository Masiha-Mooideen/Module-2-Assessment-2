CHECKERS HYPER

Checkers Hyper is a larger-format version of South Africa's fast-moving consumer goods retailer giant Checkers. It is a large-format, wider-range store with similar products to Checkers, catering for upper-income groups. The store offers an appealing selection of furniture, clothing and footwear, and much more. The store has a wider range of products than the supermarkets, including food, household items, clothing, video games, etc.
