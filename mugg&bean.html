MUGG & BEAN

Mugg & Bean is a full-service coffee-themed franchise restaurant chain that originated in South Africa. Founded in 1996 by Ben Filmalter, the concept was inspired by a visit to a Chicago coffee shop in the early 1990s. Since then, Mugg & Bean has become a beloved dining destination in South Africa, offering more than your average restaurant experience.
