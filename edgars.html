EDGARS

Edgars is a Johannesburg-based chain of stores1that sells clothing, shoes, homeware, and beauty products. It is part of the Edcon Group. The first Edgars store opened in South Africa in September 1929 in the heart of Johannesburg. Edgars provides quality fashion merchandise at competitive prices for the whole family, with no compromise on quality. The brand offers competitive credit to its customers.
